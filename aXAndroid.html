Aesthet(X) Fitness Calculator App

for Android

This is where my journey started. Written in Java, the Aesthet(X) Fitness Calculator app is a result of applying years of weightlifting experience and critical thinking. The interface was designed to make natural bodybuilding as straightforward and as visual as possible. The goal setter, set tracker, and meal planner apply mathematical modeling to bodybuilding differentiating this methodical approach from your average false promise push up and crunches app.

With this project I learned how to read through documentation, iron out bugs in the development process, and the applications of object oriented programming. A key design consideration is the AMOLED dark mode that saves battery while utilizing the app in the gym. Click here to download and view the play store listing.
